HappyFunland:

Explore the abandoned remains and Uncover the dark secrets of Mort Grisly’s Happyfunland theme park, Home of Randy Rodent! A darkly comedic VR horror experience where the fun never dies!

HappyFunland is a VR horror experience that combines immersive cinematic storytelling with dramatic interactive play. Ride the thrilling rides, solve puzzles, and defend yourself against enemy automaton robots. Can you make it home alive? No lines, no waiting, No escape!
